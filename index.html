Bizzsetu is for Indian companies catering to the communication needs related to transactions between the seller, buyer, and broker. Efficient record keeping on a single platform ensures smooth communication and record keeping.

About Us


BizzSetu is a SaaS-based (mobile app and web) communication platform which enables error free business communication between sellers, brokers, and customers in real time without disturbing their existing business model.

Problems faced using phone, email, courier etc.

Infrequent update of product information
Orders get lost between phone and WhatsApp
Disagreements in payments
Separate communication of thousands of bills to broker/buyer

BizzSetu replaces conventional methods like phone, email, fax, and courier with a single end-to-end communication platform.

A single platform for all the communication between Seller, Buyer, and their Brokers.


Do the business as you do, just change the mode of communication to make it more efficient and productive.

1) Product Catalogue

Real-time display of your products along with images to your network. You can show different products at different prices to each of your buyers/brokers.

2) Bills communication

No need to send Bills through email, WhatsApp, courier, etc. Real-time communication of Bills generated to Buyers and Brokers. Also, the facility for Buyers/Brokers to update the bill details directly into their systems, avoiding manual entries. No loss of Bills and no chances of errors.

3) Payments*

Buyer can update the payments done in real-time with auto-update in ledgers for Seller and Broker. This will result in real-time reconciliation of accounts between the transacting parties.

*Payments module is under development.
